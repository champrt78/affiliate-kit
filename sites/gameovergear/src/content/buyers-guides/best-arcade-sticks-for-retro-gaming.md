## Who This Is For

If you are coming back to fighting games or arcade compilations and want a stick you can grow into, start with one of the Sanwa-moddable wired picks. The Mayflash F300 is the lowest-cost entry and lists the widest platform compatibility, and the larger Mayflash F500 gives more room inside for parts swaps and ships with an octagonal gate. Both are built to take genuine Sanwa Denshi components later, so the cheap stick you buy now can become the stick you keep.

If you play mostly on PlayStation, the Qanba Drone 2 is the light, officially licensed entry option for PS5, PS4, and PC, while the Razer Kitsune is the premium leverless choice for players who specifically want the all-button format. On Switch, the 8BitDo Arcade Stick is the one wireless pick and doubles as a Sanwa mod platform. And if you want a tournament-grade lever with replaceable artwork and Hori's Hayabusa parts, the Hori Fighting Stick Alpha is the premium Xbox and PC option, with the caveat that its parts feel is a different ecosystem from Sanwa. Match the stick to the system you play on first, then decide how far down the modding path you want to go.
