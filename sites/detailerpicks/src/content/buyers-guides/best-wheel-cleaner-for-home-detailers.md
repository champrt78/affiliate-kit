## The doctrine split, in plain language

Two design philosophies split the wheel cleaner category, and both have credible reviewer support:

- **Iron-killer (color-changing) chemistry.** Sonax Full Effect, Meguiar's Ultimate G180124, CarPro Iron X. Thiocarbamate compounds bond to ferrous iron particles from brake dust and turn purple, red, or brown on contact. The visual feedback is the appealing feature for enthusiasts. The trade-offs: sulfur odor, higher per-ounce cost than basic surfactant cleaners, and over-frequent use is unnecessary chemistry cycling on lightly contaminated wheels.
- **Basic surfactant cleaner with corrosion protection.** P&S Brake Buster, Chemical Guys Diablo. Foaming surfactant chemistry that removes brake dust without iron-reactive color-change. The Brake Buster claim of a corrosion-inhibiting deposit layer is unique in this lineup. No sulfur odor. Lower per-wash cost at gallon pricing. The trade-off: no visual proof of iron removal, and no chemistry for embedded sub-surface iron particles (which is where Iron X comes in as the periodic decon step).

A secondary doctrine split runs through the gel-versus-spray format choice. Gel concentrates (Diablo, Brake Buster) cling to vertical wheel surfaces longer, reducing chemistry waste and increasing dwell time on barrel and spoke surfaces. Spray RTU formulas (Sonax Full Effect, Iron X, Meguiar's Ultimate) are faster and require no dilution prep, but may run off vertical surfaces before full dwell completes. For a dedicated wheel-cleaning session with a brush, gel format is the better technical pick. For quick maintenance washes or foam-cannon use, diluted spray concentrate wins.

The third doctrine line is the coated-wheels-safety filter. Every product in this guide is pH-neutral or pH-balanced and safe on coated, anodized, polished, and powder-coated finishes per manufacturer documentation. Avoid products with acid components (acid wheel brighteners are a different category not covered here) and avoid high-alkaline formulas like Meguiar's Hot Rims for anything other than painted-only wheels.
