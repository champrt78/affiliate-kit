Editor's note · no purchase needed

Use newspaper on the windshield. Yes, really.

Modern ink doesn't transfer to glass anymore. Paper is mildly abrasive without scratching. Spray your glass cleaner, wipe with crumpled newspaper, throw the paper away. Works better than most microfiber towels, costs nothing.
