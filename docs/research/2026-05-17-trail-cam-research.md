## Cell Cam Clash — 6-brand showdown (different reviewer)

Reviewer ran a 6-camera shootout: Moultrie, Tactacam, Spypoint, Spartan, Stealth Cam, Bushnell. Surfaced the **Moultrie Mobile Edge 2 Pro** as "by far the number one camera based on specs alone":

- **Activity tab** showing movement patterns based on wind, temperature, moon phase
- **Forecasted deer movement** for "your patch of timber" (paid app upgrade) — distinguishes from generic regional forecast apps like Deercast
- GPS enabled, lockable closure, no-glow IR, 0.3 second trigger, 100ft range
- Folding antenna, easy-aim mount built into back of camera (tilt up/down)
- **Auto-connect to 4 carriers** (most competitors have 2 — typically AT&T + Verizon; Moultrie covers more)

**Reviewer's caveat — important:** He explicitly does NOT recommend buying a fleet of Moultrie Mobile yet. Reasoning: previous Moultrie products burned him. His advice: "I'd really want to know how others' experiences were... not YouTubers but just people that you know or people on a group chat that have experience with these. I got burned and these cell cams are a different level of technology than just a regular trail cam... you're really committing yourself to something when you get a cell cam because you got to purchase the data plans."

**Worth carrying into our piece:** the Moultrie story is a textbook example of "amazing specs ≠ trustworthy buy." Goes well with our comparison-and-fit voice doctrine.

**Source:** [Cell Cam Clash - Moultrie VS. TactaCam VS. SpyPoint VS. Spartan VS. StealthCam VS. Bushnell](https://www.youtube.com/watch?v=A0_Jz-CDgSc)
