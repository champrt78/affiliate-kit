# Drying towel research notes — 2026-05-29

Source-attributed research for the detailerpicks buying guide "Best Drying Towels for Cars." Every spec below traces to a page retrieved this session via Firecrawl (Canopy exhausted). Amazon picks validated by scraping the `/dp` page and reading the product's own H1 title; brand-store picks validated by confirming a live product page + price. No memory specs.

A note on a trap caught during research: Amazon `/dp` pages embed "related items" carousels whose titles and specs (GSM, dimensions, color) sit inline in the scraped markdown and are easy to mis-attribute to the product you actually linked. The Gauntlet listing (B083KPZR9C) had a neighboring Liquid8r entry ("1100 GSM, 25x36, Aqua Blue") that does NOT belong to the Gauntlet. Every GSM/size/weave below is pinned to the exact ASIN's own product block, not a carousel neighbor.
