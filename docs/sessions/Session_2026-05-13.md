- **namechk.com is unreliable on Instagram.** It showed `@semperfistudios` clean ‚Äî IG signup confirmed it was taken. Trust the actual platform's signup field, not third-party aggregators, when the stakes matter.

- **TikTok's display-name cooldown is 7 days, username cooldown is 30 days.** Ray hit the username cooldown because he created the TT account on 2026-05-12 ‚Äî earliest swap is 2026-06-11.
